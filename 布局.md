## 总结

- `<ui-resizehandle>` 必须直接插在两个 `<ui-rest>` 之间
- 父容器需为 `display: flex`
- 已引入 `js/ui.js` 和 `css/ui.css`
- 不要嵌套在 `<ui-content>` 内部

  

Checklist:

- ui-resizehandle must be a direct child of ui-content, placed between two flex siblings (your ui-rest panels).

- Do not set display:none on any of these; they must be rendered to compute sizes.

- The body must have is="x-ui-" (you already have it).

- Keep jquery.browser and uidash.js loaded before using the layout.

- Place the handle between two siblings (ui-area or ui-rest) inside ui-rest > ui-content.
- Do not hide the handle or parents with display: none.
- Either add data-label (Cockpit’s way) or give the handle a small fixed width.
